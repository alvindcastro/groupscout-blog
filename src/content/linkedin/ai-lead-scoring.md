A $1.2M warehouse build is just a data point.

A hotel sales manager needs to know:
- Who is the General Contractor?
- How big is the crew?
- Is it an out-of-town crew looking for lodging?
- Should I call them today?

A human expert infers this. Claude does it automatically for GroupScout.

**How it works:**
1. **The Lead:** Raw permit data (contractor, value, location).
2. **The Prompt:** "You are a lead analyst for a hotel. Evaluate this permit for construction crew lodging."
3. **The Score:** Claude returns a JSON object with a priority score (1–10) and a plain-English rationale.

"Priority 9: Large industrial build near YVR — likely out-of-province crew."

By using Claude Haiku (fast and cheap), we enrich leads for fractions of a penny.

We don’t just find leads. We prioritize them.
