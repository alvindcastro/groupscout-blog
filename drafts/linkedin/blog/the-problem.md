If you wait for a construction crew to call your hotel, you have already lost the business.

When a $20M warehouse project starts, the coordinator has already selected a hotel for their 50-person crew.

To win this business, you must be proactive. You need to call the moment the project is permitted.

The data is public. Most cities publish weekly building permit reports. But there is a catch—they are almost always PDFs.

That is the problem I am solving with Group Scout. It:
1. Scrapes weekly permit PDFs.
2. Filters for commercial projects above a certain value.
3. Uses an LLM to estimate crew size and project duration.
4. Delivers a prioritized list to the sales team.

This creates an 8-12 week window to win the lead before the search begins.

The signal is there, but it is buried in a PDF. Group Scout finds it.
