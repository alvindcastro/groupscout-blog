Construction permits are not the only way to find high-value hotel leads.

My wife, a hotel sales director, pointed out another signal: film and TV productions.

A feature film can bring three hundred crew members to a city. For a hotel, this signal is perfect:
1. Large groups with defined timelines.
2. Limited flexibility on location.
3. Rapid mobilization.

The data hide in obscure 'In Production' lists on public websites. I found a Salesforce endpoint that serves the HTML I need.

My existing pipeline—deduplication, LLM enrichment, and Slack delivery—handles these signals with only a prompt change.

Permits show what might get built. Film and TV show who is mobilizing.
